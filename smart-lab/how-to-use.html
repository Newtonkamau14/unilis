Student Guide

Getting Started as a Student

Follow these steps to make the most of your laboratory experience with UNILIS SmartLab.

  1. 1. Login to Your Account

    Use your registration number and password provided by your institution to access the system.

  2. 2. View Your Dashboard

    Check your dashboard for upcoming lab sessions, pending reports, and recent activities.

  3. 3. Attend Lab Sessions

    Join scheduled lab sessions by checking the schedule and attending your assigned sessions.

  4. 4. Create Lab Notebooks

    After each lab session, create a digital notebook to document your observations and results.

  5. 5. Submit Reports

    Complete and submit your lab reports before the deadline. Include all required data and analysis.

  6. 6. Check Grades

    View your graded reports and feedback from your lecturers to improve your performance.

Lecturer Guide

Managing Laboratory Sessions

As a lecturer, you have powerful tools to manage lab sessions and track student progress.

  1. 1. Schedule Lab Sessions

    Create and schedule lab sessions, assign laboratories, and set session parameters.

  2. 2. Manage Student Attendance

    Track student attendance and participation in lab sessions.

  3. 3. Review Student Notebooks

    Monitor student progress by reviewing their digital lab notebooks in real-time.

  4. 4. Grade Reports

    Evaluate student reports, provide feedback, and assign grades efficiently.

  5. 5. Track Performance

    Monitor class performance and identify students who may need additional support.

  6. 6. Generate Reports

    Create comprehensive reports on student performance and lab utilization.

Administrator Guide

System Administration

As an administrator, you oversee the entire laboratory management system.

  1. 1. User Management

    Create and manage user accounts, assign roles, and set permissions for students, lecturers, and staff.

  2. 2. Laboratory Management

    Configure laboratory settings, manage equipment, and ensure proper resource allocation.

  3. 3. Asset Tracking

    Monitor laboratory assets, track inventory levels, and manage equipment maintenance.

  4. 4. System Monitoring

    Monitor system performance, review audit logs, and ensure data integrity.

  5. 5. Report Generation

    Generate administrative reports on system usage, performance metrics, and compliance.

  6. 6. Security Management

    Manage system security, handle user authentication, and ensure data protection compliance.
