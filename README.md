### ZK + CRDT Integration:
There is a lack of prior research on the communication of
zero-knowledge proofs via CRDT-based synchronization. This is particularly challenging due to
the transparency requirements of CRDTs and the lack thereof in zero-knowledge proofs as a
verification tool.
### Mobile ZK Performance: 
Current zero-knowledge proof benchmarks are largely focused on
server-based and desktop environments. There is minimal research on the performance of
zero-knowledge proofs on WebAssembly environments on mobile devices with 2 GB of RAM.
###  Byzantine Resistant Educational CRDTs: 
Educational data has integrity requirements that are
not met in current CRDT frameworks.
### Responsible Computing in ZK Systems: 
This includes energy efficiency on battery-powered
devices and the right to erasure versus the immutability of blockchain-based systems.
### Project Contribution: 
This project fills the gap in the uncharted realm of zero-knowledge proofs
and conflict-free replicated data types to prove that high-level enterprise privacy and network
robustness can indeed be achieved simultaneously in resource-constrained environments
worldwide, from rural Kenya to remote India, from refugee camps to underserved cities.
